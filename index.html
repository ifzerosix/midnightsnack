You awaken in the middle of the night craving a snack. The story can be won in as little as 30 moves, but one misstep along the way could result in a cascading comedy of errors and a night of insomnia.

Midnight Snack was created with Inform and has IFID 17AC035B-3CF1-4AF9-ABD0-AD6D4C2701B5. To play a work like this one, you need an interpreter program: many are available, among them Gargoyle for MacOS, Linux or Windows; Spatterlight for MacOS; Windows Frotz or Windows Glulxe for Windows. The postcard was written by Andrew Plotkin and designed by Lea Albaugh. Or you can play without downloading anything by following the 'Play In-Browser' link, using the Quixe interpreter. You'll need to have Javascript enabled on your web browser.
